**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Working with EC2, EBS, Security Groups](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Learning VPC basics (Subnets, Route Tables, IGW)](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Designing a small VPC architecture](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Learning S3 Advanced Features](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Working with Databases (RDS & DynamoDB)](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Learning Load Balancing & Auto Scaling](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Messaging & Event-driven architecture](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Learning Lambda & API Gateway](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Infrastructure as Code (CloudFormation)](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [AWS Monitoring & Logging](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Architecture, Security & Optimization](1.12-week12/)
