**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting Started with AWS, IAM & Billing](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Networking: VPC Architecture, Subnets, Route Tables & Security Groups](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Compute & Scaling: EC2, EBS, Load Balancing & Auto Scaling](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Storage & CDN: S3, Static Website Hosting & CloudFront](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Database: RDS (SQL), DynamoDB (NoSQL) & Multi-AZ](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [General Review & Integrated Lab Practice (VPC + EC2 + RDS + S3)](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Serverless: Lambda Functions & API Gateway](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Containers: Docker, ECR & Amazon ECS Fargate](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Infrastructure as Code (IaC) with AWS CDK](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [DevOps: Building CI/CD Pipelines (CodeBuild, CodePipeline)](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [System Monitoring & Logging with CloudWatch & CloudTrail](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Final Review: Integrating Serverless, Containers & DevOps, Workshop 1](1.12-week12/)
